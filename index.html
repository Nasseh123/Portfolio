To further my education, I attended night school at the Computer Pride at CBD Naiobi, where I learned the valuable lesson that the best teachers aren't necessarily found at the most prestigious Universities. I earned my Various Certifications in Database Administration and basic COding in various programmin languages while working full time... and quite frankly I think most folks should do likewise. Most of my work has in one way or another related to making things easier for people who use computers - both for the end users and for programmers (my work with IBM's Lombardi Edition today).
